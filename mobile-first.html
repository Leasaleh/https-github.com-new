Mobile-First Development

Mobile-First Development is a designing strategy where we prioritize designing for smaller screens and then progressively for larger screens. This ensures for smaller screen users such as smartphones users to have the same experience as larger divices users.[9] This idea came after more people began using the Internet on their phone rather than on their computers.

Importance of the Mobile-First Development

Mobile-First Development is important because it insures the improvement the accessibility and usability on mobile devices, it makes sure that the user interface is perfect for smaller screens. It also insures better performance and load times so the users, this helps slow-loading websites to improve their load time and performance on mobile devices.[10]

Principles of the Mobile-First Development

Mobile-First Development have several key steps that should be followed to optimize your device to the fullest. Wireframing is important for the visual structure and the flow of the website. Content Inventory is importent to have a clear overview of all the content. Moreover, expanding toutch targets is important because fingers are wider than a cursor so it is important to make sure that the interactive elements are large enough to tap easily.[11]
